System Readiness

Star Citizen is demanding. Ensure your rig is ready for the 'Verse.

Component Minimum (Playable) Recommended (Smooth)
Operating System Windows 10 (64-bit) Windows 10 / 11 (64-bit)
Processor (CPU) Quad-Core CPU (Intel i7 or AMD Bulldozer/Ryzen 5) Quad/Eight-Core, High Clock Speed (i7 or Ryzen 7 class)
Memory (RAM) 16 GB (Bare Minimum) 32 GB+ (Highly Recommended)
Graphics Card (GPU) DirectX 11 compatible with 4 GB VRAM DirectX 12 compatible with 8 GB+ VRAM (e.g., RTX 3060 / RX 6700 XT)
Storage SSD Required (100GB+ free space) NVMe SSD (For best performance)

IMPORTANT: The game is in active development and performance varies. An SSD and 32GB of RAM are strongly advised for a stable experience.
